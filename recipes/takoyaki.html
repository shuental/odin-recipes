Takoyaki

Japanese pancake balls made from flour with an octopus filling


Ingredients


  • Octopus
  • Mentaiko
  • Mochi
  • Avocado
  • Edamame
  • Corn

  1. Place your flour mixture to the takoyaki pan.
  2. Place the cabbage and other vegetables in each pan.
  3. Place the octopus.
  4. Flip each takoyaki to the side.
  5. Then serve.
